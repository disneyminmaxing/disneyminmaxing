<section class="section-card">
  <h2>The Core Min-Max Rules</h2>
  <div class="grid cols-2">
    <div class="card accent-gold">
      <h3>Choose your first 2 to 3 rides the day before</h3>
      <p>Do not burn rope-drop brainpower deciding what “sounds fun.” Decide in advance and use the morning to execute.</p>
    </div>
    <div class="card accent-land">
      <h3>Get to the park early</h3>
      <p>Waiting outside can save hours inside. The trade is almost always worth it if rides are a top priority.</p>
    </div>
    <div class="card accent-sea">
      <h3>Use the app as soon as you scan in</h3>
      <p>That is when you start stacking ride access, show requests, and lunch pickup windows instead of reacting late.</p>
    </div>
    <div class="card accent-gold">
      <h3>Protect lunch before lunch protects you</h3>
      <p>Mobile ordering early is one of the easiest quality-of-life wins in the whole resort.</p>
    </div>
  </div>
