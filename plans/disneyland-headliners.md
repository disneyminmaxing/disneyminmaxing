      </div>
      <p>While walking, grab the strongest available timed return for the next ride that would otherwise explode later in the day.</p>

    <div class="plan-stage">

        <span class="plan-kicker">Morning</span>
      </div>
      <p>This is where the plan gets cleaner than the old version. You are not wandering. You are filling gaps with rides that fit the geography and usually punish you less.</p>

        <a class="ride-link" href="https://www.tokyodisneyresort.jp/en/tdl/attraction/detail/183/" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer">Star Tours</a>

        <li>Use this stage to stay productive without crossing the park aimlessly.</li>
        <li>If your protected return time opens sooner than expected, cash it in immediately.</li>
      </ul>
    </div>
    <div class="plan-stage">

        <span class="plan-kicker">Late Morning</span>

    <div class="plan-stage">
      <div class="plan-stage-header">
        <h3>Stage 5: Spend the saved time on quality, not random lines</h3>
        <span class="plan-kicker">After Lunch</span>
      </div>
      <p>Once the biggest waits are behind you, use the afternoon for shows, kids’ zones, re-rides, or shopping instead of panic-queueing.</p>
      <ul class="plan-notes">
        <li>Book lunch early with Mobile Order.</li>
        <li>If your group still has energy, add <a href="https://www.tokyodisneyresort.jp/en/tdl/attraction/detail/175/" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer">Roger Rabbit’s Car Toon Spin</a> or another family ride nearby.</li>
