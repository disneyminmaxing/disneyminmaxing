  <p class="eyebrow">Sample Plan</p>
  <h1>Tokyo Disneyland Headliner Plan</h1>
  <p class="section-intro">This is a sample plan for groups who care most about high-demand attractions and want to trade some flexibility for a stronger ride count early.</p>
</section>

<section class="section-card">
  <h2>Best For</h2>
  <div class="grid cols-3">
    <div class="card accent-land">
      <h3>Priorities</h3>
      <p>Beauty and the Beast, Baymax, Splash Mountain, Big Thunder, Monsters, Inc., and Pooh’s Hunny Hunt.</p>
    </div>
    <div class="card accent-gold">
      <h3>Group style</h3>
      <p>Adults, older kids, or families happy to move fast in the morning and relax later.</p>
    </div>
    <div class="card accent-sea">
      <h3>Tradeoff</h3>
      <p>Less wandering, fewer spontaneous detours, better results on the rides that get ugly waits fastest.</p>
    </div>
  </div>
</section>

<section class="section-card">
  <h2>Sample Route</h2>
  <div class="timeline">
    <div class="timeline-step">
      <span class="time">Before Open</span>
      <strong>Arrive early and commit to your first ride.</strong>
      Do not use the entry plaza to debate the plan.
    </div>
    <div class="timeline-step">
      <span class="time">Open</span>
      <strong>Rope drop Beauty and the Beast.</strong>
      If that is not your group’s number-one goal, replace it with the headliner that matters most to you.
    </div>
    <div class="timeline-step">
      <span class="time">Immediately</span>
      <strong>Claim your next app window.</strong>
      Baymax, Splash Mountain, Monsters, Inc., or Pooh’s Hunny Hunt are the kind of targets this plan is trying to protect.
    </div>
    <div class="timeline-step">
      <span class="time">Morning</span>
      <strong>Chain nearby wins.</strong>
      Add Star Tours, Stitch Encounter, or smaller attractions while waiting for your next timed return.
    </div>
    <div class="timeline-step">
      <span class="time">Late Morning</span>
      <strong>Cash in your protected return window.</strong>
      Then look for one more strong ride before lunch.
    </div>
    <div class="timeline-step">
      <span class="time">After Lunch</span>
      <strong>Use the saved time on Big Thunder, shows, or shopping.</strong>
      This is the moment where the early discipline starts feeling good.
    </div>
  </div>
</section>
