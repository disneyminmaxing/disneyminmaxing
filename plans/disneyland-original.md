<section class="section-card">
  <h2>Day Before</h2>
  <div class="grid cols-2">
    <div class="card accent-land">
      <h3>Pick the park and know your entry advantage</h3>
      <ul>
        <li>Choose which park you are visiting before the day starts.</li>
        <li>If your group has a hotel benefit, event perk, or special early-entry option, use it. Even 15 minutes can matter a lot.</li>
      </ul>
    </div>
    <div class="card accent-gold">
      <h3>Prepare the app and your targets</h3>
      <ul>
        <li>Download the Tokyo Disney Resort app and load everyone’s tickets into it.</li>
        <li>If multiple people have the app, share tickets so more than one person can help manage the day.</li>
        <li>Pick your first 2 to 3 rides ahead of time. This is one of the most important decisions.</li>
        <li>If you like checking queue patterns ahead of time, use a wait-time site as a planning aid.</li>
      </ul>
    </div>
  </div>
</section>

<section class="section-card">
  <h2>Day Of</h2>
  <div class="timeline">
    <div class="timeline-step">
      <span class="time">Early</span>
      <strong>Get there before opening.</strong>
      <p>In your original experience, arriving around 7:30 to 7:45 and waiting outside was worth it because it saved a much bigger amount of line time later.</p>
    </div>
    <div class="timeline-step">
      <span class="time">Scan In</span>
      <strong>Use the app immediately.</strong>
      <p>Once your tickets are scanned, start handling ride access, show requests, and other reservations while moving toward your first ride.</p>
    </div>
    <div class="timeline-step">
      <span class="time">First Move</span>
      <strong>Walk to your first headliner and book at the same time.</strong>
      <p>Your original move was heading toward <a href="https://www.tokyodisneyresort.jp/en/tdl/attraction/detail/197/" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer">Enchanted Tale of Beauty and the Beast</a> while locking in your next attraction through the app.</p>
    </div>
    <div class="timeline-step">
      <span class="time">Keep Cycling</span>
      <strong>Set timers and keep checking.</strong>
      <p>The big idea from your prompt is still right: if you forget to monitor your next booking opportunity, you leak a lot of efficiency.</p>
