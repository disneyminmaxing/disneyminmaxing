<section class="section-card page-hero">
  <p class="eyebrow">Park Menu</p>
  <h1>Tokyo Disneyland vs. Tokyo DisneySea</h1>
  <p class="section-intro">If you are choosing between the parks, this page is your shortcut. Disneyland is easier to optimize for a classic ride-heavy day. DisneySea is more atmospheric and usually feels more unique.</p>
</section>

<section class="section-card">
  <div class="park-heading">
    <h2>Tokyo Disneyland</h2>
    <span class="tag land">Classic Magic</span>
  </div>
  <div class="grid cols-3">
    <div class="park-card">
      <strong>Fantasyland / nearby</strong>
      <a href="https://www.tokyodisneyresort.jp/en/tdl/attraction/detail/197/" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer">Enchanted Tale of Beauty and the Beast</a>, <a href="https://www.tokyodisneyresort.jp/en/tdl/attraction/detail/174/" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer">Pooh’s Hunny Hunt</a>, <a href="https://www.tokyodisneyresort.jp/en/tdl/attraction/detail/172/" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer">"it's a small world"</a>, <a href="https://www.tokyodisneyresort.jp/en/tdl/attraction/detail/164/" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer">Peter Pan’s Flight</a>, and several castle-area classics make this a key family zone.
    </div>
    <div class="park-card">
      <strong>Tomorrowland</strong>
      <a href="https://www.tokyodisneyresort.jp/en/tdl/attraction/detail/196/" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer">The Happy Ride with Baymax</a>, <a href="https://www.tokyodisneyresort.jp/en/tdl/attraction/detail/183/" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer">Star Tours</a>, and <a href="https://www.tokyodisneyresort.jp/en/tdl/attraction/detail/195/" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer">Stitch Encounter</a> are strong early-day targets depending on your route.
    </div>
    <div class="park-card">
      <strong>Westernland / Critter Country</strong>
      <a href="https://www.tokyodisneyresort.jp/en/tdl/attraction/detail/160/" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer">Big Thunder Mountain</a> and <a href="https://www.tokyodisneyresort.jp/en/tdl/attraction/detail/162/" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer">Splash Mountain</a> are major attractions where timing matters a lot.
    </div>
    <div class="park-card">
      <strong>Toontown</strong>
      Great for kids and lower-intensity afternoon energy, especially if you want to add <a href="https://www.tokyodisneyresort.jp/en/tdl/attraction/detail/175/" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer">Roger Rabbit’s Car Toon Spin</a>.
    </div>
    <div class="park-card">
      <strong>Adventureland</strong>
      Good for classics and secondary attractions once the headliner pressure drops.
    </div>
    <div class="park-card">
      <strong>World Bazaar</strong>
      Save it for shopping, weather breaks, or your exit window unless you have a specific reason to stop there early.
    </div>
  </div>
</section>

<section class="section-card">
  <div class="park-heading">
    <h2>Tokyo DisneySea</h2>
    <span class="tag sea">Best Atmosphere</span>
  </div>
  <div class="grid cols-3">
    <div class="park-card">
      <strong>Fantasy Springs</strong>
      <a href="https://www.tokyodisneyresort.jp/en/tds/attraction/detail/255/" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer">Anna and Elsa’s Frozen Journey</a>, <a href="https://www.tokyodisneyresort.jp/en/tds/attraction/detail/256/" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer">Rapunzel’s Lantern Festival</a>, <a href="https://www.tokyodisneyresort.jp/en/tds/attraction/detail/257/" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer">Peter Pan’s Never Land Adventure</a>, and <a href="https://www.tokyodisneyresort.jp/en/tds/attraction/detail/258/" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer">Fairy Tinker Bell’s Busy Buggies</a> can become the center of the day if they are your top priorities.
    </div>
    <div class="park-card">
      <strong>Mysterious Island</strong>
      <a href="https://www.tokyodisneyresort.jp/en/tds/attraction/detail/223/" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer">Journey to the Center of the Earth</a> and <a href="https://www.tokyodisneyresort.jp/en/tds/attraction/detail/224/" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer">20,000 Leagues Under the Sea</a> are signature DisneySea experiences.
    </div>
    <div class="park-card">
      <strong>American Waterfront</strong>
      <a href="https://www.tokyodisneyresort.jp/en/tds/attraction/detail/243/" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer">Tower of Terror</a> and <a href="https://www.tokyodisneyresort.jp/en/tds/attraction/detail/218/" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer">Toy Story Mania!</a> are two of the biggest draws in the park.
    </div>
    <div class="park-card">
      <strong>Lost River Delta</strong>
      <a href="https://www.tokyodisneyresort.jp/en/tds/attraction/detail/222/" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer">Indiana Jones Adventure</a> and <a href="https://www.tokyodisneyresort.jp/en/tds/attraction/detail/242/" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer">Raging Spirits</a> are strong thrill picks.
    </div>
    <div class="park-card">
      <strong>Mediterranean Harbor</strong>
      <a href="https://www.tokyodisneyresort.jp/en/tds/attraction/detail/219/" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer">Soaring: Fantastic Flight</a> is one of the toughest waits in the resort and needs intentional planning.
    </div>
    <div class="park-card">
      <strong>Mermaid Lagoon / Arabian Coast / Port Discovery</strong>
      Great for families, indoor time, weather relief, and lower-intensity pacing.
    </div>
  </div>
</section>
