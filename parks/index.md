<section class="section-card page-hero">
  <p class="eyebrow">Park Menu</p>
  <h1>Tokyo Disneyland vs. Tokyo DisneySea</h1>
  <p class="section-intro">If you are choosing between the parks, this page is your shortcut. Disneyland is easier to optimize for a classic ride-heavy day. DisneySea is more atmospheric and usually feels more unique.</p>
</section>

<section class="section-card">
  <div class="park-heading">
    <h2>Tokyo Disneyland</h2>
    <span class="tag land">Classic Magic</span>
  </div>
  <div class="grid cols-3">
    <div class="park-card">
      <strong>Fantasyland / nearby</strong>
      Beauty and the Beast, Pooh’s Hunny Hunt, it’s a small world, Peter Pan’s Flight, and several castle-area classics make this a key family zone.
    </div>
    <div class="park-card">
      <strong>Tomorrowland</strong>
      Baymax, Star Tours, and Stitch Encounter are strong early-day targets depending on your route.
    </div>
    <div class="park-card">
      <strong>Westernland / Critter Country</strong>
      Big Thunder Mountain and Splash Mountain are major attractions where timing matters a lot.
    </div>
    <div class="park-card">
      <strong>Toontown</strong>
      Great for kids and lower-intensity afternoon energy, especially if you want to add Roger Rabbit.
    </div>
    <div class="park-card">
      <strong>Adventureland</strong>
      Good for classics and secondary attractions once the headliner pressure drops.
    </div>
    <div class="park-card">
      <strong>World Bazaar</strong>
      Save it for shopping, weather breaks, or your exit window unless you have a specific reason to stop there early.
    </div>
  </div>
</section>

<section class="section-card">
  <div class="park-heading">
    <h2>Tokyo DisneySea</h2>
    <span class="tag sea">Best Atmosphere</span>
  </div>
  <div class="grid cols-3">
    <div class="park-card">
      <strong>Fantasy Springs</strong>
      Frozen, Tangled, Peter Pan, and Tinker Bell attractions can become the center of the day if they are your top priorities.
    </div>
    <div class="park-card">
      <strong>Mysterious Island</strong>
      Journey to the Center of the Earth and 20,000 Leagues Under the Sea are signature DisneySea experiences.
    </div>
    <div class="park-card">
      <strong>American Waterfront</strong>
      Tower of Terror and Toy Story Mania! are two of the biggest draws in the park.
    </div>
    <div class="park-card">
      <strong>Lost River Delta</strong>
      Indiana Jones Adventure and Raging Spirits are strong thrill picks.
    </div>
    <div class="park-card">
      <strong>Mediterranean Harbor</strong>
      Soaring: Fantastic Flight is one of the toughest waits in the resort and needs intentional planning.
    </div>
    <div class="park-card">
      <strong>Mermaid Lagoon / Arabian Coast / Port Discovery</strong>
      Great for families, indoor time, weather relief, and lower-intensity pacing.
    </div>
  </div>
</section>
